Player1 starts the game by rolling the dice...

The score on dice (except 1) will be added to current score of Active player (Player 1 initially).

After rolling the dice there will be two options:


  • Either to hold the current score : Holding the score will add the current score to total score of active player and pass the dice to other player.


  • Roll the dice again : If dice score is not 1, then the active player has choice of rolling the dice again and current dice score will be added to current score.


If Dice shows 1: Current score of active player becomes zero and dice is passed on to other player.



PLAYER WHO REACHES THE TOTAL SCORE OF 100 FIRST, WINS THE GAME...!!!
